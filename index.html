Cookies!

Cookies are small packages of information that are created by the website and stored in the browser. These packages are often used to identify your browser so that the next time you visiting the website it will recognize it. For example, when you visit facebook for the second time Facebook will look for any saved cookies in the users browser.

A cookie can be used for a variety of reasons for example: shopping carts, logins or analyzing user behavior. There are also different types of cookies.
